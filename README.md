# University of Makati - Event Coordination Portal (UMak ECP)

A centralized web-based platform designed to streamline the management of university events, student participation, venue reservations, and post-activity evaluations at the University of Makati.

## Overview

The Event Coordination Portal (ECP) addresses the need for a digital workflow in handling university events. It replaces manual paper trails with a system that handles event proposals, multi-department approvals (CSOA, UFMO, GSO, etc.), student registration via QR codes, and automated attendance tracking.



## Key Features

### Event Management

- Lifecycle Management: Draft → Pending Approval → Published → Registration Open → Ongoing → Completed.

- Approval Workflow: Automated routing of event proposals to specific departments (CSOA, UFMO, CIT, GSO, OHSO, SPMO) based on logistical requirements.

- Logistics Request: Request sound systems, projectors, cleaning services, medical teams, and furniture during event creation.

### Registration & Attendance

- QR Code System: Auto-generated unique QR tickets for registered students.

- Real-time Scanning: Organizations use the built-in camera scanner to mark attendance instantly.

- Capacity Enforcement: Automatic blocking of registrations when venue capacity is reached.

### Analytics & Reporting

- Dashboards: Real-time statistics for Admins, Organizations, and Students.

- Post-Event Evaluation: Mandatory evaluation forms for students after attending events.

- Automated Reports: PDF export functionality for student lists and venue reports.

### Communication

- Internal Messaging System: Direct communication between Students, Organizations, and Admins.

- File Sharing: Support for image attachments in chats.

- Email Notifications: OTP verification for signups and password resets.



## User Roles

### 1. Administrator (Admin)

- Dashboard: Overview of total students, events, and pending approvals.

- Approvals: Review and Approve/Reject event proposals based on department (CSOA, UFMO, etc.).

- User Management: Manage Student and Organization accounts.

- Venue Management: Add, edit, and toggle availability of university venues.

### 2. Student Organization (Org)

- Event Creation: Propose events with specific dates, venues, and logistical needs.

- Scanner: Use the built-in QR scanner to track student attendance.

- Analytics: View registration numbers and evaluation feedback.

### 3. Student

- Event Discovery: Browse upcoming events, filter by college or category.

- Registration: One-click registration for events.

- My Tickets: Access QR codes for entry.

- Evaluation: Submit feedback for attended events.
